isputnik.home

A browser-based home server app for friends and family, designed around invite-only access, simple sharing, personal notes, and a digital media library.

Our world revolves around you.

Private by default, shared when it matters, and small enough to maintain at home.

Design principles

Built for trust, not scale theatre

Self-hosted

Runs on a home server with SQLite and filesystem storage. No required cloud dependency.

Family-friendly

Invite-only accounts, clear roles, familiar sharing, and simple account recovery patterns.

Modular

Notes, library, jobs, and future tools can grow independently without reshaping the core.

Build path

Start small, then widen the orbit

  2. 02 App shell, profiles, themes
